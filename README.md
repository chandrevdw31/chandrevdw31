## About me

I'm a **born-free South African** results-driven marketing professional, especially interested in roles that combine **content strategy**, **social media management**, and **developer-focused marketing**—with **community engagement** as a strong secondary focus.
At **MindsDB** as Marketing and Community Coordinator, I worked closely with product, engineering, sales, and developer relations to **create and distribute technical content**, **run cross-platform campaigns**, and **grow developer ecosystems**—while moderating communities and supporting contributors across Slack, GitHub, and social channels.
I'm skilled in content strategy, cross-platform publishing, digital communications, and community coordination, with a track record of strengthening brand visibility and audience engagement through tutorials, launch posts, newsletters, product pages, and live events.
Passionate about technology, open source, content creation, and strategic communication—I bring a creative, proactive, and collaborative approach to every project. Following a company-wide restructuring at MindsDB, my role was impacted as part of broader organizational changes; I left on **positive and professional terms**.

🟢 **Open to opportunities** in content marketing, social media, developer marketing, technical writing, and community roles in AI/data.
